ABOUT US

At Sweet Treats (Bakery), we're dedicated to spreading joy, one delicious baked good at a time.

"Discover a world of delectable baked goods at Sweet Treats (Bakery). From classic croissants to decadent cakes, every item is a masterpiece of flavor and texture. Try our signature chocolate lava cake, seasonal fruit tarts, or custom wedding cakes. We also offer gluten-free and vegan options. Come indulge in the sweet life with us!"

This bakery was founded by Samishtha in 2015, driven by a passion for spreading joy through delicious baked goods. From humble beginnings to multiple locations, we remain committed to using only the finest ingredients and delivering exceptional quality and customer service. Our mission is to bring people together, one sweet treat at a time.
